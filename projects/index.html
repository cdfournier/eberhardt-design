ClientBright Bucks

We worked with Avani Miriyala to design and build a rewards app for iOS and Android where users earn money by performing simple tasks to save on their existing purchases.

Our Challenge
Build a rewards app for iOS and Android that is clean and easy to understand and provides a great experience for the user.
